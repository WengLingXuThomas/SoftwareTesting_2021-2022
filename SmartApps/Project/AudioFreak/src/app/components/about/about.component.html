Description

The application is a visual overview of all the music of a chosen folder primarily using the File System Access API. You can listen to your own songs while singing along with the associated lyrics, you can adjust the music with an equalizer to your own liking and watch a visualizer formed on the rhythm of the music. If you don't have local songs on your device, you can make use of the YouTube streaming tab to look up a song and listen along. You can also create your own playlists by selecting songs and adding them to the list.
Go Explore and see for yourself!
